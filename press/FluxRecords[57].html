Here, for your listening pleasure and edification is 3 timezones,
my 5th album in the Flux Records Digital series of sound, image, & text (FLX22).

V

In 2005, after once again failing to get a single staging of my new, pure electronic music in New York, I turned to the airwaves of the internet. For the first time however, I conceived of a work that exists in 3 time zones simultaneously while only in 1 locale.

So on the 3rd day of the 3rd month of the year 2005 I recorded 3 tracks at 3 different times in front of 3 internet audiences of 3 humans each in 3 time zones with each performance lasting 33 minutes and 33 seconds. 3 Times for 3 Timezones is the superimposition of all 3 as my opus 41 and now, more than 10 years later, my album 3 timeszones.

In the press release for the project I announced that ROJO & CREST will play on the data processing electronic music instrument IFORMM, which gives the impression that there are 2 performers and 1 instrument. Again, through spirit communication I split myself into 3.
