Rothkamm's additive synthesizer music K5 premieres on the Day Of Noise

On Sunday 2/12/2012 @ 12PM (Pacific Standard Time) K5 will be streamed live and for the first time on KZSU Stanford as part of the Day Of Noise festival.

K5, which took 8 years to complete, is a sequence of 8 works, ranging from 3 second "ID" nanomusics to the 9:06 minute "Threshold Magnitude".

The 33 minutes and 33 seconds of K5 represent the finest pinnacle of AdiSySciFiSe - Additive Synthesized Science Fiction Serialism.
