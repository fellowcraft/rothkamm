PRESS RELEASE

FRANK ROTHKAMM:
AMERIKA on YouTube

Tetralogy IV

Frank Rothkamm's AMERIKA is a 73 min. HD film + installation of a CD + 4to.

AMERIKA is the fourth and last part of TETRALOGY, which as a whole investigates borders and perception.

Furthering theories first laid out in the Birth Of Primary Cinema From The Spirit Of Sound[1], the film is a poetic-mystical endurance test for the medium and its message[2] - an esoteric AMERIKA that exoterically entices the YouTube self to see everything that is and not is:
(A) AND (NOT A).[3]

AMERIKA represent a supermodern antithesis to YouTube via YouTube -
a Averroesian[5] Music in 10 parts[6].
