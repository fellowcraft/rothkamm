Here, for your listening pleasure and edification is Nine Chains to the Moon,
my 23th album in the Flux Records Digital series of sound, image, & text (FLX40).

XXIII

Nine Chains to the Moon is Buckminster Fuller’s first book, originally published in 1938 in New York. I remapped this as an audiophile motion picture comprising 9 works that I produced in New York from 1996-1997. All of the 9 were commissions, they were all created as a soundtrack, remix, art installation, ritual or for a festival. Besides digital audio composition, keyboards, analog synthesizer, and drum programming, I also noodle here on the electric guitar. I am deeply indebted to Raymond Chen, DJ GLOVE, Alfred Harth, Josef Haydn, Hans Werner Henze, Max Lässer , Henry Mancini, Ellliott Sharp, Joseph Trump, and the Hermann Lodge #268 without whom none of these Nine Chains to the Moon would exist
