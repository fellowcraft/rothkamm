Rennes (France) - On July, 18 2008 the French radio show L'azile le + sur (canalb.fr) and the webzine sonhors (sonhors.free.fr) devoted a extensively researched 95 minute radio special and a grand on-line interview to the pure electronic composer Frank Rothkamm (rothkamm.com).
