Why Do Birds Suddenly Appear?

Art and Life are one idea. They are so inseparable that the one escapes commodification as much as the other, tragically in flux and never able to free themselves from the illusion that the pursuit of ever greater Mehrwert (Profit) is the same force that places a wedge between them. Like smoke on the water, Reason alone can not give birth to this concept, but frees it from the unavoidable limitations of a possible experience, separating the one and the other. We are not free birds, descendants from the antediluvian inhabitants of this planet who once roamed the earth and then took to the skies. We can, however, read the flight of birds like the ancients did, as a fugue of monads, twittering and twisting through the centuries. Never as one, yet always together.
