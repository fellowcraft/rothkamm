Is it Zeitgeist or am I Overcome By Art?

We are at the end of the 1st decade of the 21st century and yet there are no flying cars available. Here in Los Angeles, from the 10th floor of the Wilshire Royale, I can only see tiny people and tiny cars, neither of which move much in rush hour traffic. They don't fly, they don't take off and they are confined to the 2 dimensional space of the road.

We are at the end of the 1st decade of the 21st century and yet I cannot simply talk to a computer. "Hello Computer, please bring my flying car to the window". Nothing flies and moves by command alone. So, I have to pick up the cell phone and call the valet. "Hello, can I get my car please? The last digits are 594." The valet walks to the car, brings it to me, and then I drive.

We are at the end of the 1st decade of the 21st century and what was once science fiction turns out to be neither science nor fiction and certainly not reality. We became only ALT. Thus, there is still a dominant illusion of reality: intrinsically, only birds can move freely in 3 dimensions and only ghosts appear to be able to move in the 4th dimension of time itself.

We are at the end of the 1st decade of the 21st century and some new things and some new concepts have appeared. Only 4 mouse-clicks away, only for YOU, and only before this decade is OUT, here are the 4 Things For The End of it:

"Forsaking all humor - at least of the German kind - to conjure up epistemology, numerology, and the summa summarum of Zeitgeist - at least of the American kind - TETRALOGY's overarching scheme is tied to a psycho-geography that moves through New York and ends up in Los Angeles, taking alchemical ideas to extremes and suggesting, as the successor to the FB01 machine trilogy: 1) to re-examine the eternal ideas of naturally occurring, computed, played and historic forms of sound; 2) to finally fulfill a somewhat long-winded, Kantian imperative of a full 74 minutes in the 4th installment; 3) to formulate principles of a transformative supermodernism within technology; 4) and to create a spiritual nomenclature that seeks to ultimately free mankind from the temporality and mortality of the dominant illusion of reality with only 4 PLASTIC DISCS."
