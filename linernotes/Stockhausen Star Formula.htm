I

I had a dream: on the night of February 26, 2013, the late composer Karlheinz Stockhausen visited my studio to see if I could materialize one of his works.

He looked around the room somewhat critically.
"So, Frank",
he asked, while nodding his head,
"how did you pay for all of this?"
I replied proudly:
"I used to be the Senior Systems Analyst for the New York Philharmonic."
He took a seat and we started to work. I saw all of his diagrams but then I thought,
"I can't hear any sound"
and was worried.
He nodded again and I realized that I could synaesthetically see the sound in the diagrams.

Then I woke up, staring at a white piece of paper in front of me.
