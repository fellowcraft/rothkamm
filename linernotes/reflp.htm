rothkamm's REFLP is a monophonic "Historical Reference Long Player" to the FB01 Compact Disc; a compilation of 6 obscure modernist tracks from the dawn of pure electronic music in the 1950s. The LP mirrors the CD in sequence, mood, technique and structure. Although entirely digital in nature, REFLP has been especially composed to give the authentic feel of a vinyl record. rothkamm fulfills here the second imperative of supermodernism: "Nod to the first pioneers of electronic music".

[01] Paul Gredinger Formanten II Cologne 1954
[02] Franco Evangelisti Incontri di fasce sonore Cologne 1957
[03] Carlos & Bebe Barron Love at the Swimming Hole New York 1956
[04] Vladimir Ussachevsky Metamorphosis New York 1957
[05] Raymond Scott Clavivox New York 1958
[06] Herbert Eimert Glockenspiel Cologne 1953
